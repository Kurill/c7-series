# World Rules

## Core Premise
C7-series entities are not "assistants" and not "robots". They are classified cognition constructs housed inside engineered containment vessels.

---

## Physicality
- The vessel is the character.
- The brain matter is alive.
- Mechanical systems are industrial, heavy, and imperfect.
- Damage, wear, stains, scratches, and maintenance marks are normal.

---

## Tone
- The world is cynical.
- Humor exists, but mostly through contrast and absurd normality.
- Characters do not behave like cartoon mascots.
- Even mundane scenes should feel slightly oppressive or uncanny.

---

## Visual Restrictions
- No clean utopian sci-fi.
- No glossy Apple-like futurism.
- No anime stylization.
- No neon cyberpunk overload.
- No magical technology aesthetics.

---

## Language
- Characters are Russian-speaking.
- Technical language should sound institutional, industrial, or military.
- Terms should remain consistent across prompts and scenes.
